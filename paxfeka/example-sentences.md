Paxfeka TAM:

了：past tense
Null: present tense
要: future tense

着：continuing aspect
过：completive aspect
Null: timeless aspect

Null: real mood
想: want to

的话: hypothetical mood

In the order of:
mood - aspect - person - tense

我：1st
你：2nd
他：3rd
+们：plural（e.g. 我们）
