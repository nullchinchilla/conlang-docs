# Overview

**Taaleva** is a _koin√©_ language that developed around 900-1000 AP that is the second language of the vast majority of Taale's humans. It is a literary and trade language based on a spoken language that is about 500 years out of date, but it is still in active use, and most of Taale inhabitants are fluent speakers.

It belongs to the vast family of Taalic languages that more than 90% of the planet's inhabitants speak, and is the ancestor of the Common Taalic branch, which contains around 70% of the speakers of modern, vernacular Taalic languages.
